About



Hi I am Yanni. I am a user experience designer based in the Netherlands. I have just gained the Master degree of Design for Interaction in the Delft University of Technology. I like exploring subtle and novel ways to interact with the ordinary daily-life surroundings, but, in a structured and methodology-based design process. I used to think that these two issues somehow contradict with each other, but, I am trying, exploring and learning. Maybe, there is a perfect trade-off somewhere?
